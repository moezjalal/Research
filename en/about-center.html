Vision

Invest in, protect, and develop natural resources to improve agricultural and livestock production through scientific methods.

Mission

Lead in agricultural research, environmental science, and climate studies to enhance food security and ensure resource sustainability.

Objectives

1. Achieve food security

2. Expand cooperation locally, regionally, and internationally

3. Develop human resources and increase staff efficiency

4. Serve the community and environment by enhancing the center's positive values
